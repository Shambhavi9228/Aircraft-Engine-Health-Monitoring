# Results

The LSTM model was developed to predict the Remaining Useful Life (RUL) of aircraft engines.

Key Outcomes:
- Processed aircraft engine sensor data
- Developed an LSTM-based predictive maintenance model
- Predicted Remaining Useful Life (RUL)
- Supported maintenance planning through early failure prediction
